Frequently Asked Questions(FAQ)

After the terrible things happened in Las Vegas, is it save to go to the Brooklyn Outdoor Film Festival?

Naturally, we cannot guarantee your safety, but be assured, we do everything humanly possible to ensure nothing bad will happen to you.

We cooperate with the NYPD (department Patrol Boroughs Brooklyn I), the international Red Cross, the FDNY (Borough Command Brooklyn), with a direct order from the Fire Commissioner Daniel A. Nigro, to make sure, every possible health threat is taken care of.

Update (10/31/2017):

We feel terribly sorry about the latest terroristic attack, that hit New York. According to the News, the criminal has planned to hit as many people as possible and continue his deadly rampage until the Brooklyn Bridge.

The festival will take part, no matter what terrorist will do next, we won't allow ourselves to draw back. We are here and we will stay! A terroristic act is a crime against humanity, not more and not less. If we hide, we send the wrong signal. Let us stand together against all resistance.

Is the event suitable for minors?

Yes, all movies are open, for all peope, of all ages.

How much is the entrance fee?

It is free. Even sodas, non alcoholic beverages, and popcorn are free of charge. We would like to take to opportunity to thank the Duff Brewery for the great gift.

Note:Since the event is open for everybody alcohol is not allowed!

I heared the rumor that President Donald John Trump is holding the opening speach. Is that true?

We have definitely offered him the opportunity to hold the opening speech. But as of now, he did not confirm nor decline.

But, Eric Adams(Borough President of Brooklyn), a strong supporter of the festival, will give us the honor of his presence.
