About the Brooklyn Outdoor Film Festival

Dear fellow citizens,

the Brooklyn film festival exists since 1977. Yes, that is right. Since 40 years, we offer free and open movies to everybody who is interested.

Let's make a festival to remember. A festival you will tell your great children about.

Forget Woodstock. The Brooklyn Outdoor Film Festival has it all!



History:

Before 1977 John Smith Jr., Minister for Education and Fine Arts, had the desire to implement a cultural event in Brookly to rival the International Venice Film Festival. It is finally more than five years after the end of the war in Vietnam, that John said, we had enough of war. We need a festival to celebrate the beaty of life. So was the first edition of the International Film Festival in Brooklyn born.

Since 2014 the festival was called the Brooklyn Outdoor Film Festival.
